Our first project of Product, Promotion, and Packaging consisted of taking an object that we thought was well designed and served its purpose. Then, we were given the challenge to create a package for that object. This package couldn’t reveal what object was inside. Using texture, fonts, color and images we rendered a unique package that gave the viewer a clue of what was inside, the packaging had to have some sort of relationship with the product.

I chose a cardholder because i thought it was very easy and practical to carry around. The final prototype that I chose to render was a mini pocket that had multi use. After it was used as a packaging, it could latter be used as a coin holder since the cardholder doesn’t have any place to store in coins.
